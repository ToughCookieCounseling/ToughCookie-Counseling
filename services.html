CLINICAL SERVICES

Thoughtful, grounded care designed for individuals, families, and those navigating complex life demands.

Individual Therapy

Support for navigating stress, trauma, identity, and the realities that follow you beyond the moment. This work is tailored, direct, and grounded in both clinical expertise and real-world understanding.

Couples Therapy

Whether navigating first responder life or everyday relationship challenges, we help couples strengthen communication, rebuild trust, and create stability within the relationship.

Family Support

Families often carry the unseen impact of stress, service, and transition. This work focuses on connection, understanding, and creating a more stable, supportive environment for everyone involved.

Rates

Individual Therapy: $135 per session

Couples & Family Therapy: $175 per session

We believe access to care should not be limited by financial circumstances. If cost is a concern, we encourage you to reach out. We are open to discussing options and working with you to find an approach that is both manageable and supportive.
