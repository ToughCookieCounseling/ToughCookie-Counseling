Clinical

Individual Counseling

A focused space to work through personal challenges, build insight, and develop practical strategies for change. Whether you are managing stress, trauma, substance use, or life transitions, individual therapy is tailored to your goals and pace.

Couples Counseling

Support for couples navigating communication breakdowns, trust concerns, or the impact of high-stress careers. We work to strengthen connection, improve understanding, and help partners move forward with clarity and intention.

Family Counseling

A collaborative approach to improving family dynamics, resolving conflict, and strengthening relationships. Family therapy helps create stability, structure, and healthier patterns of communication within the home.

Your therapist will require speaking to all members one on one prior to a full family session.

Assessment Services

Comprehensive behavioral health assessments designed to provide clarity, guide treatment planning, and meet clinical or administrative requirements. Assessments are thorough, thoughtful, and tailored to your specific needs and circumstances.

Insurance & Superbill

Tough Cookie Counseling is currently in the process of becoming in-network with select insurance providers. At this time, we do not bill insurance directly.

Upon request, we are happy to provide a monthly superbill for you to submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. Please note that reimbursement is not guaranteed and depends on your individual plan.

We believe cost should not be a barrier to care. A limited number of reduced-rate appointments may be available based on financial need and availability. Please inquire directly for more information.

Cancellation & No-Show Policy

Your appointment time is reserved specifically for you. We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling.

  • Appointments canceled less than 24 hours in advance will be charged the full session fee.
  • Missed appointments (no-shows) will also be charged the full session fee.
  • Late arrival or leaving early does not reduce the session fee.
  • All clients are required to keep a valid credit card or HSA card on file, which will be charged on the day of service.

This policy is non-negotiable, as your scheduled time is held exclusively for you and cannot be offered to another client on short notice. Your time is valuable, and ours is as well.

Good Faith Estimate

In accordance with federal law, you have the right to receive a "Good Faith Estimate" outlining the expected cost of your care. This will be provided upon request or prior to the start of services.
