Samantha Battrell

LPCC-S | LICDC

Samantha Battrell brings a grounded, direct, and compassionate approach to therapy, shaped by both clinical experience and a deep respect for the realities people are navigating every day. Her work is especially informed by the emotional weight carried by first responders, families, and individuals who have spent a long time holding everything together.

Approach

Samantha’s style is thoughtful, practical, and deeply human. She believes therapy should be a place where people can be honest without having to translate themselves, soften their experience, or perform healing in a polished way.

Her work is rooted in respect, real-world understanding, and the belief that meaningful care must be both clinically sound and emotionally attuned.

Focus Areas

First Responders

Support for those navigating chronic stress, trauma exposure, identity strain, burnout, and the pressure of being steady for everyone else.

Families & Partners

Care for the people who stand beside those in demanding roles and often carry the relational, emotional, and practical impact of that work.

Trauma & Emotional Overload

Thoughtful treatment for people moving through overwhelm, grief, accumulated stress, or the long-term effects of experiences that were never fully processed.

Life Transitions

Space for people navigating changes in identity, relationships, work, family life, or the internal shifts that happen when old ways of coping stop working.

Clinical Style

Samantha values honest conversation, strong therapeutic connection, and care that respects complexity rather than oversimplifying it. She does not believe in one-size-fits-all therapy, and she works to create an environment that feels steady, capable, and free of pretense.

Her goal is not to force people into a version of healing that looks good from the outside. It is to help them process what hurts, understand what is happening beneath the surface, and move toward greater clarity, stability, and connection.

Credentials

Licensure

Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor with Supervision designation and Licensed Independent Chemical Dependency Counselor.

Professional Background

Experience spanning behavioral health leadership, clinical oversight, compliance, training, supervision, program development, and direct therapeutic care.

Samantha’s work is for the people who have learned how to keep going under pressure, often without enough space to set any of it down. Her aim is to offer therapy that is steady, respectful, and capable of holding what feels heavy.
