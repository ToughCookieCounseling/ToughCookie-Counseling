SAMANTHA BATTRELL

LPCC-S | LICDC
Clinical leadership rooted in real-world experience and deeply human care.

Samantha Battrell brings a grounded, direct, and compassionate approach to therapy, shaped by both clinical experience and a deep respect for the realities people are navigating every day.

Her work is especially informed by the emotional weight carried by first responders, families, and individuals who have spent a long time holding everything together.

APPROACH

Samantha’s style is thoughtful, practical, and deeply human. She believes therapy should be a place where people can be honest without having to translate themselves, soften their experience, or perform healing in a polished way.

Her work is rooted in respect, real-world understanding, and the belief that meaningful care must be both clinically sound and emotionally attuned.

FOCUS AREAS

FIRST RESPONDERS

Support for those who carry chronic stress, trauma exposure, hypervigilance, and the quiet strain that often comes with service.

FAMILIES & PARTNERS

Space for the people who love someone in a demanding role and need support, clarity, and steadiness of their own.

TRAUMA & RESILIENCE

Care that honors survival while helping clients move toward safety, self-understanding, and sustainable healing.

LIFE TRANSITIONS

Guidance through identity shifts, burnout, relationship strain, and seasons where holding it all together no longer works.
