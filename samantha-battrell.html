Samantha Battrell

LPCC-S  |  LICDC  |  LPCA

Clinical leadership rooted in real-world experience and deeply human care.

Samantha Battrell is the founder of Tough Cookie Counseling and a clinician known for her direct, thoughtful, and deeply grounded approach to care. She brings a blend of clinical expertise, operational leadership, and real-world understanding to her work with individuals, couples, and families.

  • LPCC-S (Ohio) — Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor with Supervision designation
  • LICDC (Ohio) — Licensed Independent Chemical Dependency Counselor
  • LPCA (Kentucky) — Licensed Professional Counselor Associate

Background

Her professional background spans behavioral health leadership, program development, compliance, and direct clinical care across a range of settings. She has worked extensively with individuals navigating trauma, substance use, family system challenges, and the unique pressures tied to high-responsibility roles.

Samantha has a strong connection to the first responder community, both professionally and personally. That perspective shapes her ability to engage with clients in a way that is both clinically sound and genuinely relevant to their lived experience.

Approach

Samantha's style is clear, collaborative, and focused on meaningful progress. She creates a space where clients can be honest, challenged, and supported — without pretense or unnecessary complexity.

Direct & Practical

She believes therapy should be a place where people can be honest without having to translate themselves, soften their experience, or perform healing in a polished way.

Clinically Grounded

Her work is rooted in respect, real-world understanding, and the belief that meaningful care must be both clinically sound and emotionally attuned.

At the core of her work is a simple belief: people are capable of far more than they have been given the space or support to realize.

She is especially committed to supporting the unseen weight carried by first responders and the loved ones who stand beside them.
