Clinical Supervision

Structured, supportive supervision for LPCs working toward independent licensure in Ohio. Supervision is grounded in clinical skill development, ethical practice, and real-world application — with a focus on helping clinicians grow in both confidence and competence.

Developing Clinical Judgment

Support in strengthening case conceptualization, treatment planning, documentation, ethical decision-making, and overall confidence in practice.

Reflective Supervision

Space to think through difficult cases, emotional responses, countertransference, boundaries, and the weight clinicians often carry in the work.

Practice Readiness

Guidance for clinicians who want supervision that is not only compliant, but also useful in preparing them for sustainable, capable long-term practice.

Supervision Rates

Individual Supervision $60  /  1 hour
Group Supervision $30  /  1.5 hours

Supervision meets state requirements and includes regular individual and/or group sessions, documentation review, and guidance aligned with Ohio Counselor, Social Worker, and Marriage and Family Therapist Board standards.

Approach to Supervision

Supervision is offered with the same values that shape clinical work: honesty, steadiness, practical support, and respect for the complexity of the role. The goal is to help clinicians grow into thoughtful, grounded professionals — not simply meet minimum requirements.

Internships

Internship experiences should offer more than observation. They should provide meaningful exposure, strong mentorship, and opportunities to grow in both skill and confidence.

Training Future Clinicians

We offer internship placements for graduate-level counseling and social work students seeking hands-on experience in a supportive, clinically focused environment. Interns gain exposure to real-world practice, including assessment, treatment planning, documentation, and client engagement.

All internships are structured to meet university and state requirements, with consistent supervision, clear expectations, and opportunities to build practical skills that translate beyond the classroom.

  • Currently only accepting Interns with CT status in Ohio.
  • Internships are a paid position. Please reach out for details.

Work With Us

Tough Cookie Counseling is always looking to connect with clinicians who value autonomy, integrity, and meaningful work.

Independent Contractor Status

All clinician roles are structured as 1099 independent contractor positions. This model offers flexibility and autonomy, allowing clinicians to manage their own schedules and caseloads within a supportive, compliant framework.

Ideal Candidates

We are looking for clinicians who are grounded, self-directed, and committed to serving first responders, families, and the broader community with intention and professionalism.

Clinician Responsibilities

Contractors are responsible for maintaining their own licensure, liability insurance, and tax obligations in accordance with state and federal guidelines.

To express interest, please email us at toughcookiecounseling@gmail.com .

CEU Development & Training

We design and deliver continuing education and training opportunities tailored to the real-world needs of clinicians, first responders, and organizations.

Behavioral Health Topics

Training content including trauma, substance use, ethics, family systems, and clinical topics relevant to behavioral health professionals.

First Responder-Focused Education

Development of training and educational content specific to the mental health realities of first responders and the family systems around them.

Flexible Delivery

Trainings can be offered as individual courses, group sessions, or customized programs for agencies and teams — meeting state CEU requirements where applicable.

Training Philosophy

Professional development should be useful, relevant, and rooted in the realities clinicians are actually navigating. The strongest education leaves people better equipped, more thoughtful, and more capable in the work they return to afterward.

Professional support at Tough Cookie Counseling is built around the same core standard as the rest of the practice: thoughtful work, real-world usefulness, and respect for the people carrying the responsibility.
