About

I’m Samantha Reynolds-Battrell, LPCC-S, LICDC — a clinical leader and therapist with a passion for supporting individuals navigating high-stress roles, trauma, and complex life transitions.

My approach is grounded in practical, evidence-based care combined with a deep respect for the lived experience of each person I work with. I specialize in working with first responders, professionals, and families who carry more than most people see.

Therapy with me is direct, supportive, and focused on helping you regain control, clarity, and stability.

Services

Individual Counseling • First Responder Support • Clinical Supervision • Consulting

Services are tailored to meet the needs of high-functioning individuals facing burnout, trauma exposure, and major life stressors.
