About

Introduces the heart behind the practice, the training behind the work, and the steady hand clients can expect when they arrive.

Samantha Reynolds-Battrell, LPCC-S, LICDC

I’m a therapist, clinical leader, and founder committed to helping people navigate high-pressure roles, trauma exposure, and life transitions with clarity and steadiness.

My work is especially focused on first responders, professionals, and families who often function well on the outside while carrying heavy, unseen stress underneath.

My therapeutic style is direct, compassionate, and practical. I believe people deserve care that respects both their resilience and their pain, without forcing them to choose between the two.

FAQ

What should I expect in a first session?

We’ll talk through what brought you in, what feels hardest right now, and what you want help changing. The first session is about clarity, not pressure.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes. Therapy is confidential with standard legal and safety-related exceptions, which will be reviewed clearly at the beginning of services.

Do you work with first responders?

Yes. This is a primary focus of the practice and an area of deep professional and personal understanding.
