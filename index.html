Welcome!

At Tough Cookie Counseling, we specialize in supporting those who carry more than most. Our work centers on first responders, the individuals who stand beside them, and the families shaped by both the demands and the meaning of that role.

We provide therapy for first responders navigating the weight of service, couples both within and outside of first responder life, and families seeking stability, connection, and understanding. Whether you are facing the visible challenges or the quieter ones that follow you home, our approach is grounded, thoughtful, and tailored to the realities you live every day.

This is a space where resilience is respected, complexity is understood, and real support begins.

Why Us?

Many of our therapists bring lived experience as first responders and as partners, parents, children and loved ones who share in that life. We understand the culture, the pressure, the dark humor, the unspoken expectations, and the weight that does not clock out at the end of a shift.

This is a unique fluency.

Our team is deeply committed to serving the first responder community and those connected to it. We recognize that support does not stop with the individual in uniform. It extends to the relationships, the households, and the quiet spaces where the impact of the work is often felt most.

Because of that, our approach is both clinically sound and context aware. We do not ask you to translate your world for us. We meet you in it, with respect, clarity, and a genuine dedication to helping you and your family move forward with strength and stability.
