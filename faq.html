FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

A few common questions about therapy, telehealth, and getting started with Tough Cookie Counseling.

What is therapy?

Therapy is a space to talk honestly, reflect more deeply, and work through what feels heavy, overwhelming, or stuck. It can offer support, insight, and practical tools for navigating stress, trauma, relationships, life transitions, and the parts of life that feel hard to carry alone.

Do you take insurance?

We are currently in the process of credentialing with several insurance providers. At this time, services are offered on a private-pay basis while paneling is being finalized.

We are happy to provide a superbill upon request for potential out-of-network reimbursement, depending on your plan.

Do you offer telehealth services?

Yes, we offer secure telehealth sessions for clients located in Ohio and Kentucky. Telehealth provides a flexible and accessible option for care while maintaining the same level of quality and confidentiality as in-person services.

How do I get started?

The easiest way to get started is to visit the Contact page and reach out by phone or email. From there, we can talk through next steps, availability, and whether the practice feels like the right fit for your needs.

What if I am not sure therapy is the right fit?

That is completely okay. Many people reach out with questions before making a decision. Starting with a conversation can help clarify what you are looking for and whether this feels like the right place to begin.

Is what I share confidential?

Yes. Your privacy is taken seriously, and services are provided with confidentiality in mind, subject to the legal and ethical limits required by law.
